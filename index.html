Sport training is a systematic process
aimed at enhancing athletic performance through a combination
of physical conditioning, skill development, and mental preparation.
It typically involves a well-structured regimen that includes various
workouts, drills, and practice sessions tailored to an athlete's specific sport.
Effective training not only focuses on strength, endurance, and agility but also
emphasizes recovery and injury prevention. Mental strategies like visualization
and goal setting are key for maintaining focus and resilience.
